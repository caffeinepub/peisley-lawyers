## Implementation Plan

### Pages
1. **Home** -- Hero section with firm name and tagline, brief overview of services, calls to action (Free Legal Aid, Veterans Assistance, Contact)
2. **About** -- About Peisley Lawyers, commitment to justice, background and values
3. **Practice Areas** -- Criminal law specialisations: defence, serious crimes, summary offences, appeals
4. **Free Legal Aid** -- Explanation of free legal aid eligibility, how to apply, what is covered
5. **Veterans Assistance** -- Dedicated legal help for Australian veterans, types of matters handled, how to access
6. **Contact** -- Contact form (name, email, phone, message), office details (placeholder), map placeholder

### Technical
- React multi-page app with React Router for navigation
- Consistent header/navbar and footer across all pages
- Professional dark navy and gold colour palette
- Fully responsive layout
- No backend data requirements -- static content site
- Contact form submits to backend (store enquiries)
